Homemade Sarma


Description:

Sarma is a traditional dish made of cabbage leaves stuffed with a flavorful mixture of minced meat, rice, and spices.
It is commonly enjoyed in many Eastern European and Middle Eastern countries, often served with a dollop of sour cream or yogurt.

Ingredients:

  • 1 large head cabbage, frozen for 3 days, then thawed overnight in the refrigerator
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • ½ pound ground pork
  • ½ pound ground ham
  • 1 cup uncooked long-grain white rice
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon coarse ground black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 pound sauerkraut
  • 1 cup tomato juice
  • 1 cup water, or as needed to cover
